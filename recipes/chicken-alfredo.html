Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken Alfredo is a popular Italian-American dish consisting of fettuccine pasta tossed in a creamy Alfredo sauce, with pieces of chicken added to complete the meal.

Ingredients

  • uncooked fettuccine
  • boneless chicken brest halves
  • jar Alfreo-style pasta sauce
  • frozen vegetables
  • 1/3 cup milk

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring it to a rolling boil. Cook the fettuccine at a boil until it's tender but also firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain well.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, place cube cooked chicken, Alfredo sauce, frozen vegtables, and milk in a large saucepan over medium-low heat. Cook and stir until chicken is heated through and vegetables are tender
  3. Serve warm Alfredo and chicken sauce over cooked noodles.
